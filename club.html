EXAWO fighting club

Exawo Fighting is a member of Exawo group, which is the fitness and sports company. Everyone from age of 6 is welcome to come and train with us. At our club there is no such a thing as "beginners course". We train together all the time, it does not matter if you've just started or are the black belt! During the session we do some basic techniques, lots of power, speed and stamina exercises, practise kata and sparring. We attend training camps, participate in various competitions. Insurance is the responsibility of the student!
